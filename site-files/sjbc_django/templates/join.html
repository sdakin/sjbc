Become a Member

Drop on by whenever we’re open!

Anyone can drop on by the San Jose Bike Clinic at our shop at 19 N 2nd St in downtown San Jose where we are open Monday through Thursday from 5 pm to 8 pm. The day rate to use the shop is $5. If you are short on funds, there are opportunities to earn shop usage through volunteering. Please email us at sjbc@bikesiliconvalley.org if you have any questions.

What you get from dropping by the SJBC:

  • Free training on how to fix a bike
  • Donate to receive extra help on your own bike
  • Get to know other bicyclists of all skill levels!
  • Use of SJBC space and tools
  • Help build our community and improve the health of bicycles in San Jose through sharing your bike building knowledge.
